Anthony Romero

Founder & CEO

Origins & Education

Born in 1985 in the Dominican Republic, Anthony Romero pursued his passion for science and engineering, earning a Bachelor's of Science in Chemical Engineering.

Professional Background

His career began in the pharmaceutical industry, where he demonstrated his entrepreneurial spirit by establishing and running his own medicine manufacturing company. His expertise later extended to paint development, working with major paint manufacturers.

Construction Journey

After moving to Canada in 2012, Anthony recognized the immense potential in the construction sector. Over the past 12 years, he has built a successful construction business that continues to grow and evolve.

Current Success

As of 2024, Anthony's leadership has established RCO as a respected name in the industry, maintaining strong relationships with global real estate companies and successfully managing commercial building maintenance and renovation projects.
